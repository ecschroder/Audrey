I'm Audrey Schroder.

I'm a Community Manager & Blogger-in-Chief for the leading electronic cigarette company, Vapor4Life.

I studied Public Relations at Ferris State University in Big Rapids, Michigan. For three years, I served as the Chapter President of NOW (National Organization for Women) and was an active member of PRSSA (Public Relations Student Society of America).

After earning my B.S. in Business, I left the small town for Chicago-- the city in which I had always dreamed of living.

When I'm not staring at a computer screen, I like organizing, exercising at my local Y, reading, and driving my boyfriend crazy by re-arranging the furniture.
